# Privacy Policy - LinkedIn Feed Blocker

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## Overview

LinkedIn Feed Blocker ("the Extension") is a browser extension designed to help users block the LinkedIn feed during specified times. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

The Extension collects and stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Settings preferences**: Your blocking schedule, time preferences, and feature toggles
- **License key**: If you've purchased a license, the key is stored locally for validation

### What We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect browsing history
- We do NOT collect personal information
- We do NOT track your LinkedIn activity
- We do NOT sell or share any data with third parties

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Chrome's `chrome.storage.sync` API. This means:
- Data stays on your device and synced Chrome browsers
- Data is encrypted by Chrome
- We cannot access your stored data

## License Validation

When you enter a license key, we may validate it against our licensing server. This validation only transmits:
- The license key you entered
- No personal information is transmitted

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Retention

Your settings are stored until you:
- Uninstall the extension
- Clear your browser data
- Reset the extension settings

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (view your settings in the extension)
- Delete your data (uninstall the extension or clear settings)
- Opt out (simply don't use the extension)

## Children's Privacy

This Extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
