Frequently asked questions

How do I install the extension?

Install LinkedIn Feed Blocker from the Chrome Web Store, then open LinkedIn in Chrome. Click the extension icon in your toolbar to open settings.

How does feed blocking work?

The extension hides the LinkedIn home feed so you can use LinkedIn without endless scrolling. Jobs, Messages, profiles, and other pages are not affected — only the main feed is blocked.

In the extension popup, choose Always block to hide the feed whenever you visit it, or Schedule-based to block only during the time windows you set (before work, during work, after work, and optionally on weekends).

Can I temporarily view the feed?

Yes. If bypass is enabled in settings, click 5 min break on the blocking overlay to view the feed for five minutes. You can end the break early from the timer shown on the page.

The feed isn't being blocked. What should I try?

  • Confirm the extension is enabled in chrome://extensions
  • Make sure you're on the LinkedIn feed (linkedin.com/feed or the home page)
  • Check that Block Feed is turned on in the extension popup
  • If using schedule mode, confirm the current time falls within a blocked period
  • Refresh the LinkedIn tab after changing settings
  • Make sure you're using Google Chrome on linkedin.com

Is my data collected?

Your settings are stored locally on your device. We do not collect browsing history or LinkedIn activity. Read our full Privacy Policy for more information.
